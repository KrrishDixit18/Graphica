About AIT Pune

All India Shri Shivaji Memorial Society's Institute of Information Technology, Pune (AIT Pune) is a premier institute in the field of Information Technology and Computer Science. Established in 2003, AIT Pune has consistently been ranked among the top engineering colleges in India. The institute is known for its strong academic curriculum, experienced faculty, and state-of-the-art infrastructure.
